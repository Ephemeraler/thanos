1. IN-MEMORY
```yaml
type: IN-MEMORY
config:
    max_size: <string>
    max_size_items: <int>
    validity: <duration>
```

对应

```yaml
enable_fifocache: true
fifocache:
    max_size_bytes: <string>
    max_size_items: <int>
    validity: <duration>
```
